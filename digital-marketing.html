Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between on-page and off-page SEO?

On-page SEO focuses on optimizing elements within a website itself, such as content, HTML tags, and internal linking. Off-page SEO, on the other hand, involves activities outside of the website, such as building backlinks from other websites, social media engagement, and online reputation management.

What are the different types of SEO?

The different types of SEO include:

  • On-Page SEO: Optimizing individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic.
  • Off-Page SEO: Building external signals like backlinks and social media mentions to increase the authority and trustworthiness of a website.
  • Technical SEO: Improving the technical aspects of a website such as site speed, mobile-friendliness, and crawlability to enhance its performance in search results.
What are backlinks and why are they important for SEO?

Backlinks are links from other websites that point to your website. They are important for SEO because search engines view backlinks as a signal of a website's authority, credibility, and relevance. The more high-quality backlinks you have from authoritative websites in your industry, the more likely it is that your website will rank higher in search results.
